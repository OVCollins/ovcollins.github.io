The Background

Journalist first.
Everything else follows.

Collins Victory Odabi is a Nigeria-based multimedia professional whose work sits at the intersection of journalism, digital strategy, and audio storytelling. His editorial instincts were forged early — as the founding President and Editorial Board Chairman of the Association of Campus Journalists at the University of Benin, Benin City, Edo State, he built structures and standards, not just content.

Today, he hosts three distinct podcasts spanning professional development, Web3 infrastructure, and the emerging economics of direct air carbon removal. Each show reflects a consistent philosophy: disciplined curiosity, rigorous preparation, and a commitment to bringing specialist knowledge to broader audiences.

Beyond audio, Collins serves as Technical Director at Christ's Chosen Church of God International (CCCGi). He previously served as Director of Strategic Communication and PR at the National Association of University Students (NAUS) South-South VP office. His background spans digital marketing, graphic design, freelance writing, and substantive engagement with crypto markets and Web3 ecosystems.

The Shows

Three voices.
Three distinct worlds.

Interview · Professional

On Your Mic

An interview-based podcast that extracts operational insight from practitioners across business, leadership, law, the arts, and professional practice. Every episode is built around a single guest and conversations that span a range of themes — from the mechanics of leadership to the quiet decisions that define careers.

FORMAT: Audio · Interview · 20–30 min
APPROACH: Insight-focused
Analysis · Web3

Web3 Unfiltered

An analytical podcast for builders and thinkers inside the Web3 ecosystem. There are no hypes and no surface-level takes here — only infrastructure-level conversations about where decentralised systems are actually going, and why it matters to the people building them.

FORMAT: Audio · Analytical · 30–50 min
AUDIENCE: Web3 Builders & Thinkers
Enterprise · Climate

The SkyCarbon Enterprise Hour

A show examining direct air carbon removal as an economic opportunity across the American West. The conversation draws in policymakers, scientists, and enterprise leaders who are actively shaping the carbon economy — and asks the questions that determine whether it scales.

FORMAT: Audio · Enterprise · Climate
FOCUS: Carbon Removal & Climate Economics
